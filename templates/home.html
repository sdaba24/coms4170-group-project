IN FORMATION

Formations in soccer are the positions of the players on the field, ranging between offensive, defensive, and balanced between the two. Coaches pick the formations before the game starts based on their players' strengths, their opposition's strengths, and whether they want to protect leads.

Formations are read in order of defense to offense, i.e. the first number is the line of players closest to the goal keeper. Players stay within their line as they move up and down the field.
